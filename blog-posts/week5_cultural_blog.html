Pairing:

What was it like to pair with others to solve challenges?

Frankly pairing with other people to solve challenges is fun! It offers a way of swapping tips and tricks while enjoying another person's company and also swapping war stories.

Was something particularly fun or rewarding?

Getting to know my cohort mates better, swapping specific tips and tricks, and just hanging out was very fun.

Was something frustrating and difficult for you?

Nothing particularly frustrating or difficult so far about pairing.

Feedback

No

Difficult - most of the folks I've paired with have been really great; there's not much actionable advice I can offer them.

Overall, what do you think of using pairing and feedback to guide your learning?

Overall, pairing has been amazingly helpful; although the feedback process has not been as helpful thus far.
