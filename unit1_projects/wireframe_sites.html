Comparison of Wireframes

Hacker News

Hacker News provides news stories and interesting content for hackers, or people who are interested in finding interesting solutions for interesting problems. Literally, there are no visual challenges being solved here - this is a pure case of a website with a design that completely gets out of the way of the conte of the website.

Farnam Street

Farnam Street is a blog site that provides the best summary of knowledge and information based on articles and books that have been written in the past. There are also no visual challenges being solved here - this is a pure case of a website with a design that completely gets out of the way of the conte of the website.

Spotify

Spotify is a website that advertises a streaming music service directed mainly to consumers. Spotify aims to bring visitors in with their visual style - like many consumer websites, the focus here in on an aesthetic, engaging website that is flashy, cool and keeps people's attention.
